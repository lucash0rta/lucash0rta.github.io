Cyber-Feminist Speculative Future



A Short animation and poem riffing off of cyberfeminist literature and art.


  • Explored cyberfeminism concepts through influential texts and artworks.
  • Developed ideas and imagery inspired by cyberfeminist pioneers.
  • Created mood boards, researched, and refined concepts.
  • Executed 3D modeling, texturing, and dynamic animation.
  • Enhanced realism using Unreal Engine's live link for motion capture.
  • Utilized software tools: Metahumans, Mixamo, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Blender.
