3D-ME



Exploring personalised avatars for use on 3d social platforms using facial scans and photgrammetry.


  • Utilised photogrammetry to create a detailed recreation of facial features and used Photoshop to stitch the layers together and touch up details to ensure even lighting and remove any artifacting from the photogrammetry process.
  • Remodelled and optimised the output geometry.
  • Modeling of clothing models.
  • Rigging and animating the character.
